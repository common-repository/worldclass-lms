== Description ==

Worldclass adds a powerful layer to your existing WP site that turns your site into a fully functional online academy and LMS.

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fq3bEUaT6ts]

Here are some of our powerful features:

* **Manage your users**   
  We provide your with hosted signup and login services. Allow guest signups, access by role/group, support social logins, manage users, assign users to groups, advanced user profiles with full timelines of activities and much more..

* **Create advanced online courses**  
  Use our intuitive drag-and-drop editor to create stunning learning experiences - use HD videos, presentations, images or embed html content.

* **Build advanced online assessments**  
  Worldclass comes fully packed with TestCore ™ - the most powerful test creation suite in the universe!
  Create advanced tests and quizzes using any imaginable question type. Set time limits, randomize questions, draw by difficulty and much more. You can create any type of assessment from self-paced learning to adaptive, real-world tests using common templates such as GMAT, SAT, LSAT, TOEFL

* **Sell subscriptions online**  
  Worldclass comes ready out of the box with advanced commerce capabilities. No setup needed! You only need to set your prices for pro membership or per-course and start collecting payments.

* **Launch your own mobile app!**  
  Done setting up your content? Happy with the results? Get your brand on the appstore! Worldclass allows you to launch a fully branded, native mobile application for your users on iOS and Android. Let your users study on the go with your own mobile academy!  
  *Note: you can use the Worldclass app with your academy free of charge or launch your own branded app for a monthly fee. Check out our pricing page at [www.worldclass.io/pricing](http://www.worldclass.io/pricing/) or contact us at [sales@worldclass.io](mailto:sales@worldclass.io) to find out more.*
  
* **Get full visibility into your business and users**  
  Worldclass automatically collects data about your users and complies them into rich reports and dashboards. You get any level of visibility from individual learners status, profile and detailed event streams to academy wide reports you can filter by course, group or topic.
  

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

* **Is Worldclass free?**
  YES! You get all the core features of Worldclass for free. The free license is reserved for users of the Worldclass WordPress plugin up to 150 active users or 20 courses. To go beyond these limits contact us at [sales@worldclass.io](mailto:sales@worldclass.io) or via our website at [www.worldclass.io](http://www.worldclass.io)

* **To activate the plugin, I need a valid Worldclass.io academy. How do I set one up?**  
  Go over to our [signup page](http://studio.worldclass.io/#!/signup), create a Worldclass account and go back to your WP admin panel. Simply paste your academy’s URL and off you go.

* **How can I build courses and tests?**  
  Head over to [studio.worldclass.io](http://studio.worldclass.io) and use our full editor environment to manage your courses and users or change the appearance and settings for your academy and mobile apps.

* **How can I manage user access to my academy?**  
  All aspects of user access, role management, payments and settings can be managed via your academy admin panel at studio.worldclass.io

* **Have more questions? Need help getting started?**  
  Please do not hesitate to contact us at [support@worldclass.io](mailto:support@worldclass.io) and let us help.


== About Worldclass.io ==

Worldclass.io is a mobile first, "Do-It-Yourself", cloud based SaaS, enabling users to create their very own branded e-learning environment, and publish highly interactive courses on a fully branded mobile app & website.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `worldclass` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Worldclass' menu in your WordPress admin panel.

For basic usage, you can also have a look at the [plugin homepage](http://worldclass.io/WordPress/).
